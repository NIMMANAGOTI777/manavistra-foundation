Why We Started

Manavistra Foundation was born from a simple realization: many people want to help, but don't know how. We wanted to bridge that gap. We started as a small group of friends distributing food on weekends, and today, we are a movement.

Our core values are Transparency, Empathy, and Immediate Action. We don't just provide aid; we restore hope.

Note: Our official NGO registration is currently in process. All activities are conducted with full transparency and community support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Manavistra Foundation a registered NGO?

We are currently in the process of official registration. However, our groundwork has been active for over a year with full community support and transparency.

How can I donate?

You can donate via UPI, Bank Transfer, or by scanning the QR code in the "Donate" section. We accept contributions for specific causes like blankets or food.

Will I get a receipt for my donation?

Currently, we provide a simple acknowledgement receipt. Automated 80G tax-exemption receipts will be available once our registration process is complete.
